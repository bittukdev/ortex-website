Our History

Our Story

Ortex Industries has established itself as a leading manufacturer of customized t-shirts, caps, and promotional apparel in India. With years of experience in the garment manufacturing industry, we have built a reputation for quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

Our journey began with a vision to provide businesses and organizations with high-quality customized apparel solutions. Today, we serve a diverse range of clients including corporate companies, government organizations, educational institutions, political parties, NGOs, and retail brands.

Located in New Delhi, our modern manufacturing facility is equipped with advanced machinery and operated by skilled professionals who ensure every product meets international quality standards.
