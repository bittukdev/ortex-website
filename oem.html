The Process

From Concept to Your Branded Product

Our streamlined 6-step manufacturing process ensures transparency, quality, and on-time delivery for every order.

01

Consultation & Brief

Share your brand vision, product requirements, target audience, and budget. Our team creates a tailored manufacturing plan.

02

Design & Tech Pack

We develop production-ready tech packs with fabric specifications, color codes (Pantone matching), sizing charts, and construction details.

03

Sampling & Approval

Receive physical samples before bulk production. Review fit, fabric, print quality, labels, and packaging — approve or request modifications.

04

Bulk Production

Full-scale manufacturing with dedicated production lines. Multi-stage quality checks at cutting, stitching, printing, and finishing stages.

05

Quality Control & Packaging

6-point quality inspection followed by custom packaging with your branded polybags, boxes, hang tags, and care labels.

06

Shipping & Delivery

Domestic and international shipping with tracking. Export documentation, customs support, and doorstep delivery — PAN India and worldwide.
